XENONIUM therapeutic device

for inhalation of medical xenon

The product is nominated to the IF DESIGN AWARD 2022

Xenonium is a therapeutic device for inhalation of medical xenon. The device can provide procedures in an automatic mode of operation that keeps setting parameters of the procedure. Our team designed a colorful, modern, and safe medical device body, provided easy access for changing gas cylinders, and ensured device mobility.

Engineering tasks

  • engineering composition and planning of the structure;
  • support of industrial designers;
  • design the low-cost device frame;
  • choose the materials for prototyping and production;

    • Design engineering process

  • The device consists of base frames that take all load on themselves, the head device with a sensor control monitor, an open place for gas cylinders, and the blue-lighted door.
  • The base frame is a standard aluminum construction profile. These tubes are easy to assemble and inexpensive. Also, they are lighter than welding tubes.
  • We added a door for easy access to the device interior and maintenance. The door has tough offset hinges installed on the frame.
  • For vertical stability, the product has an extensive bottom base that consists of a thick metal plate.
  • Even though it is a big device with huge oxygen and xenon cylinders, we made it mobile. The doctor or specialist can easily move it. The device can be moved and rotated via enduring design case handles and wheels with stoppers.

    • To sum up

    The device has a user-friendly ergonomic design. Also, our team provided easy access for gas cylinder changing, came up with secure assembly solutions. That keeps the device in a safe and reliable functioning condition for a long-term period.
